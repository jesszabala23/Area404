Night at the Aquarium

Spend the night with us at our Georgia Aquarium Sleepover! We’ll enjoy guided activities, behind-the-scenes tours, and admission to some of the aquarium’s special shows.

The fall 2020 sleepover has been cancelled. We’ll reschedule for fall 2021. All the information that follows will be updated when details are finalized.

We’ll check in at the aquarium on Wednesday evening for orientation, then enjoy behind-the-scenes programs and an evening snack. We’ll have breakfast and morning activities until 10:00 am on Thursday, then have the rest of the day free to spend at the aquarium. This sleepover is for children ages 5 and up (age verification will be required) and their parents and grandparents. Tours are targeted to different age levels, and teens and college students enjoy it just as much as the younger children.

Cost and What’s Included

The cost of this event is $70 per person (students and adults), payable by debit or credit card. Payment is due immediately, and you’ll be directed to my online payment system after you submit your registration. (Registering without paying will not hold spots for your family.)

Your fee covers your entry into the aquarium, all educational programs and activities, admission to the 4D FUNBelievable Theater, Under the Boardwalk sea lion show, AT&T Dolphin show, an evening snack* on Wednesday, and breakfast* on Thursday.

Parking is an extra $12.

Cancellations and Refund Policy

Payment is refundable only if I have a family on my waiting list to replace you, and a $30 per-person cancellation fee will be deducted from any refunds. Your spots aren’t transferable, and can’t be transferred to a future sleepover date. No replacements, roster changes, or refunds can be made after July 31 for any reason (including medical issues, sickness, death in the family, etc.), so please register with this in mind.

Your signed waiver, registration, and payment acknowledges your understanding and agreement with my cancellation and refund policy.
