About Our Team

Welcome to Area404, a curated directory of interesting places and events throughout Metro Atlanta's.

This site was created by Harsha Javle, Nia Smith, Nia Van Buren, and Jessica Zabala as an exercise in the application of Frontend web development fundamentals for the Techbridge Technology Career Program (TCP) in partnership with Goodwill of North Georgia.

Harsha Javle

Harsha Javle is an emerging web developer with an educational background in computer science.

I am new to Web development and recently learned about Front end development using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and jQuery. My background education is in computer science but I have not persued a career in it for a long time. I started learning front-end web development four weeks back. Here is my collaborated effort with three of my wonderful teammates to design, develop, and deploy this Area404 website. Feel free to browse through and don’t forget to give us feedback.

Apart from this new effort of learning the most updated technology, I am also a full-time homemaker and mother of two boys.

In my free time, I enjoy traveling, reading, cooking, and volunteering in the community.

Nia Smith

I recently migrated from Jamaica to the United States last December. Before I migrated,I worked as a part-time fashion model I wanted a career switch once I migrated and the TechBridge program is the best choice I’ve made, as it’s a creative field where I’ll be able to express my creativity digitally.

In my free time, I love hiking with my friends and family, going thrift shopping and learning to cook new and unique dishes from my cousin.

Nia Van Buren

Nia Van Buren is a Full-Stack Digital Content Specialist with a background in applied linguistics.

I began my training in the field of applied linguistics, earning high marks in topics spanning phonetics, phonology, semantics, and syntax at Georgia State University.

I hope to parlay my knowledge of syntax and written structure into web development with a focus on consistency and Quality Assurance.

I aim to continue toward a dedicated study of Machine Learning Artificial Intelligence (AI).

Jessica Zabala

Jessica Zabala is a creative first-generation American with over 8 years in marketing and TV production throughout LA, Miami and most recently Atlanta.

I'm quietly confident, ambitious, and perpetually developing my design techniques in all mediums. I am a strong believer in dynamic styling and pride myself in making pages come to life.

I aim to further my career as a full-stack developer alongside clients that are focused on making viable differences in their communities and the world. As someoone who has spent years working in industries where I would be the only woman on set my intention is to bring woman to the foreground as well as prove that immigrants come to the United States to make vital contributions to this economy and are necessary for a greater future.
